About Shushant Singh Rajpoot (SSR)

Shushant singh rajpoot was an Indian actor known for his work in hindi cinema. He starred a number of commercilly successful bollywood movies such as M.S.Dhoni: the untold story, Kedarnath and Chhichhore etc.

Born: 21
Died: 14 June 2020 (aged 34), Bandra west, Mumbai
Height: 1.83m
Parents: Mr.K.K.Singh, Usha Singh
Active years:2008-2020
Cause of death: Suicide by hanging
First movie: Kai po che! (2013)
Last movie: Dil bechara (2020) (released after death)
